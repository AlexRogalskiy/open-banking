Catalogue

This is a catalogue of APIs from the specifications authored by the open banking implementation entity under the directive from the United Kingdom's Competition and Markets Authority. The APIs are free to use for experimentation. This environment contains simulated data and limited support. Additionally APIs from other bodies such as STET and BIAN are be considered in the future.
