Open Banking Sandbox

Open banking has captured the imagination of digital leaders and is emopowering customers to use new digital services with their banking information. Harnessing the power of platform that is open source, is helping banks to be in control of their own destintity and accelerating innovation.

Create an ecosystem, connect with your customers or simply make it easier to partner with you

Explore prefabricated APIs and data to create digital applications based upon open banking specifications

The APIs are built on a microservices architecture and deployed In a cloud-native platform based on specifications from the United Kingdom and others.

Our goal is to make it easy to take advantage of these standard APIs without any strings attached. We can also provide the sandbox to banks who are looking to accelerate their open banking platform with enterprise open source capabilities while including their own brand and data, and having it run and managed by Red Hat as part of an OpenShift Dedicated service is an option.

The sandbox is fully open source and available on GitHub.

Catalog

The catalog is inclusive of the standard specifications from the United Kingdom and Australia.

  • Account and Transactions
  • Payment Initiation
  • Open Data
  • Event Notification
  • Event Subscription
  • Confirmation of Funds
  • Consumer Data Protection
New specifications will likely be introduced in the future and existing specifications will be updated periodically.
